Our Specialized Services

Counseling: Overseas Education

Our counseling services do not stop with helping you choose the right course, country or university perfect for you. Our services extend to helping you make your right career selection and follow up with the education that rightly fits your plan. Or Admission Advisors are at hand, to help you shortlist the relevant courses in the countries and universities that meet your specific career objective.

Prepare: Tests & Selections

At Eurobase, we have a connection with hundreds of universities in over 15 countries across the world. We are well aware of the process and the requirements of the universities in those countries. So while we help you select courses in relation to your career goals, we assist Admission processes. We have the facility to prepare you for tests, find the right financial aid and help you with visa processing.

Select: Course, University and Country

For most students who have made up their mind on the course of action for the future, all that is left to do is select the right course. We at Eurobase help you with choosing the right course at the right university. While you shortlist the universities, you will also be choosing which country to move to. The factors to take into account would include scholarships, paid internship programs, etc.

Apply: Admission and Decision Assistance

While all other issues are in your favor, the final deciding factor for getting admission to a university will be your application. It is how you are introduced to the admission committee that makes the difference. At Eurobase, we assist you with the drafting of an effective Statement of Purpose (SOP) as well as the Motivation Letter. We help you prepare your Resume and Essay which helps in your selection.

Fly Abroad: Visa Processing

After you get your admission, the remaining step is Visa Processing. As you are aware, different countries have different visa requirements and it takes a special skill to process it. The visa processing is pretty intricate and involves filling the visa application and forms and getting all the necessary documents in order. We at Eurobase can help you with mock visa interviews and help you sail through.

Value: Additional Services

Educational service encompasses many specialties. At Eurobase, we offer a comprehensive service that includes a whole range of services. We can help you with accessing the right Educational Loan, help you with Forex, ensure the Travel plans and provide you assistance to find the right accommodation in a foreign country. We help you get a feel of the lifestyle and culture of that country too.
